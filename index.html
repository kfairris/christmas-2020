The Fairris Family

2020 has been a different kind of year hasn't it?

March led to the ending of Alee's work and the start of virtual school for the kids. Kenny's office closed so he has been working from home since March. It has been a big adjustment for all of us. Alee and the kids faced challenges adapting to virtual school, but they found a groove and made it through the spring. We found a new pool to join this summer since our regular one didn't open, and it was a blessing to our family.

We've seen grandparents a little this year, but not as often as we would like to. We were thankful for the kids to get to go to school in person this fall. The kids have excelled after being back at school with friends, and it has been good for us as a family to have a little bit of normalcy.
